# Archival Announcement

This document contains the public announcement text for the archival of
`opencode-cc-camouflage` and its upstream dependency `not-claude-code-emulator`.

---

## Twitter / X Announcement

### English

> `not-claude-code-emulator` has been archived. As of ~midnight KST 2026-04-06
> the emulation layer stopped fooling Anthropic's auth checks, which was its
> sole purpose. `opencode-cc-camouflage`, the patch companion that depended on
> it, is archived as well. Thanks everyone who tested and filed issues. It was
> a good run. 🪦

### Korean (한국어)

> `not-claude-code-emulator`가 보관 처리됐습니다. 2026년 4월 6일 자정(KST)을
> 전후해 앤트로픽 인증을 속이는 에뮬레이션이 동작을 멈췄고, 이것이 해당
> 프로젝트의 유일한 존재 이유였습니다. 이에 의존하던 패치 컴패니언
> `opencode-cc-camouflage`도 함께 보관 처리합니다. 테스트하고 이슈 남겨주신
> 모든 분들께 감사드립니다. 좋은 여정이었습니다. 🪦
